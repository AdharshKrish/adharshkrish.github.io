About The College

Pondicherry Engineering College is an Institution promoted and fully funded by the Government of Puducherry. The College was started in 1984. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and College development and is managed by a Board of Governors. The College has been growing from strength to strength since its inception. Pondicherry Engineering College is a partner Institute in TEQIP-III , which aims at Twinning Arrangements to Build Capacity and Improve Performance of Participating Institutes. PEC is ranked 76 in Engineering Category by NIRF India Rankings 2019. More info...







About Cybyrus

Cybyrus is a national level symposium conducted by the vibrant students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Pondicherry Engineering College.Every year we relaese the department magazine which exhibits various technical and non-technical ideas of building engineering students of our department.Students all over India get to participate in this event.

  • Author's Guideline
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must be registered for the conference to appear in the proceedings and be scheduled for presentation
  • For "Fee Structure" kindly refer the Registration Link
  • Send the paper in .doc/.docx Format
  • Refer "Sample Paper template” from the conference website.
  • All participants are required to submit registration form and payment proof in compressed (zip) format via email to iciccas2020@pec.edu
  • Important Note
  • Participants MUST bear all banking charges.
  • Registration fees do not include accommodation and travel expenses.
  • All accepted and presented papers will be published in conference proceedings with ISBN number.
  • All high quality research papers will be considered for publication in UGC / SCOPUS /SCI Indexed Journals with additional charges.
  • No refund will be made after the completion of Registration Process
